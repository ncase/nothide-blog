Show & Tell #1 - Let There Be Light

Saturday, September 28th 2013

Nothing to Hide, Nothing to Fear: a surveillance state survival horror.

A new game project, started literally just this week! Next week, I'll flesh out the line-of-sight mechanics, but for right now, I've programmed a Monaco-inspired shadow-masking effect.

Please ignore the placeholder catgirl.

I posted this GIF and other screenshots to /r/gamedev in their weekly Screenshot Saturday thread. Out of curiosity, I asked them if they'd like a tutorial on how to make a similar shadow effect.

The response?

Very well! I want to make this game as open and transparent as possible. Making tutorials and behind-the-scenes posts about once a week would be a good start, I think. The goal is for everything to be open: its development, its design, even its funding.

Fits the title of Nothing to Hide, no?

P.S: GIF was recorded using LICEcap, an awesome open-source tool. I would have otherwise resorted to taking 15 screenshots a second and manually turning it into a GIF. Seriously.
